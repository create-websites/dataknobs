KREATE: Create What Matters

The KREATE module is the generative core of the Dataknobs platform, providing a suite of tools designed to build the foundational assets of any data product. It empowers users to create everything from high-quality datasets and AI-powered websites to sophisticated conversational agents. This pillar embodies the "Data-Centric AI" philosophy by focusing on engineering high-quality, AI-ready assets as the primary driver of performance. It's the starting point of a virtuous cycle, where well-crafted assets are passed to the KONTROLS and KNOBS modules for governance and refinement.

KONTROLS: Focus on Guardrails and Risks

The KONTROLS module represents the governance and risk management backbone of the platform. It provides comprehensive guardrails for AI-driven data products, ensuring that assets created with KREATE are developed and deployed responsibly. This framework moves beyond a simple checklist, offering a multi-layered, holistic approach to AI governance that spans the entire product lifecycle, addressing critical enterprise concerns around data privacy, algorithmic bias, model transparency, and security. It ensures that innovation does not come at the expense of trust and compliance.

KNOBS: Enable Diagnostic and Experimentation

The KNOBS module provides the optimization and experimentation layer, enabling users to diagnose performance and iteratively improve their data products through systematic testing. It embodies the idea of democratizing system tuning by providing intuitive, abstract controls—or "knobs"—that allow users to influence and test complex AI systems without deep engineering expertise. This creates a powerful feedback loop, where insights from experiments can inform the next cycle of creation in the KREATE module, driving continuous improvement.

The KREATE Product Suite

Within the KREATE pillar, Dataknobs offers a powerful suite of products designed to automate and accelerate the creation of data-driven assets. Each product targets a specific stage of the creation process, from engineering data to building AI agents. Use the tabs below to explore each product's capabilities.

KreateDatasets: Engineering High-Quality Data

Positioned as the cornerstone of the platform's "Data-Centric AI" philosophy, KreateDatasets is an engineering environment for manufacturing high-quality data assets. It uses advanced techniques like Active Learning, Generative Models, and Weak Supervision to create and curate data. A key capability is synthetic data generation, which is crucial for scenarios where real-world data is scarce, sensitive, or imbalanced, effectively solving the "cold start problem" for many AI projects.

KreateWebsites: Content-First Web Experiences

KreateWebsites inverts the traditional website building process. Instead of starting with a template, users provide their content from sources like Google Drive or PDFs, and the AI engine generates a complete, SEO-optimized, and high-performance website. This content-first approach targets users who prioritize speed, automation, and search engine performance over the granular control of traditional drag-and-drop builders. It even uses AI agents to automatically refresh content, keeping sites dynamic and relevant.

KreateBots: Advanced Conversational AI

KreateBots is a comprehensive framework for building a wide spectrum of conversational AI, from simple chatbots to complex, reasoning-driven AI agents. It is designed for rapid, low-code development and offers unparalleled flexibility. The platform is LLM-agnostic (supporting OpenAI, Google, etc.), Vector DB-agnostic (Pinecone, Chroma DB), and supports deployment on-premise or on any major cloud. Its robust Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG) system allows bots to provide accurate, context-aware answers from private knowledge bases.

The KONTROLS Governance Framework

The KONTROLS module provides the critical governance layer for enterprise AI. Its value is in providing a trust and safety framework that is essential for regulated industries. This section breaks down the multi-layered approach Dataknobs takes to ensure AI is developed and deployed responsibly.

Data-Centric Kontrols

Ensures data quality and security through lineage tracking, pre-processing pipelines, and privacy measures like data masking and anonymization.

Model-Centric Kontrols

Provides oversight of the ML model lifecycle, including bias detection, performance monitoring, and version management for rollbacks.

Output-Centric Kontrols

Focuses on the results generated by AI, implementing user feedback loops and automated ethics and compliance checks on model outputs.

Infrastructure-Centric Kontrols

Secures and optimizes the underlying environment with auto-scaling, cost monitoring, disaster recovery, and end-to-end encryption.

Governance-Centric Kontrols

Manages overarching policy with detailed audit trails, comprehensive documentation, and strict Role-Based Access Control (RBAC).

Business & User-Centric Kontrols

Bridges the gap to business value by tracking ROI and ensuring AI products are aligned with user needs and enhance satisfaction.
