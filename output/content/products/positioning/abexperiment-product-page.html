One Platform for All Your Experiments

ABExperiment is a unified A/B testing and experimentation platform that empowers product teams to optimize user experiences across websites, chatbots, LLM-powered applications, and data/AI models – all in one place.

Explore Features

A Unified Approach to Optimization

From static web pages to dynamic AI interactions, test everything under one roof with a consistent, data-driven methodology.

Unified A/B Testing Across Channels

Test a new webpage design one day and an AI chatbot’s dialogue strategy the next, using one consistent interface. Eliminate siloed tools and view all results on a central dashboard to make data-driven decisions without juggling multiple systems.

Dynamic Chatbot & AI Assistant Experiments

Optimize dynamic conversations by testing different dialog strategies, tones, and conversational flows. Track specialized metrics like goal completion, conversation length, and user satisfaction to systematically improve your conversational UX.

Experimenting with LLM Applications & Data Models

Compare different LLM backends (OpenAI, Gemini, Claude), vector databases, or embedding models in a controlled environment. De-risk changes in your AI stack by validating model performance on metrics like accuracy, latency, and cost-per-query.

Agentic AI for Automated Experimentation

Leverage autonomous AI agents to automatically generate variant designs and simulate user interactions. Get preliminary results overnight without waiting for real user traffic, accelerating your testing cycle and reducing risk.

LLM-Powered Analysis for Faster Insights

Turn raw data into actionable insights instantly. Our platform uses LLMs to interpret statistical results, explain them in plain language, and generate implementation-ready reports, freeing your team to focus on strategy.
