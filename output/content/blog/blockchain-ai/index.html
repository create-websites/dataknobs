Blockchain

A decentralized, distributed, and immutable digital ledger used to record transactions across many computers so that the record cannot be altered retroactively without the alteration of all subsequent blocks.

Artificial Intelligence

A branch of computer science dealing with the simulation of intelligent behavior in computers. It enables machines to learn from experience, adjust to new inputs, and perform human-like tasks.
