The Blockchain Solution: A Single Source of Truth

Blockchain introduces a shared, unchangeable ledger that every participant in the supply chain can view and trust. In this section, we compare the old way with the new, blockchain-powered approach to illustrate the fundamental shift towards transparency and security.

Traditional System

Data is siloed in separate, private databases. Information is passed from one party to the next, with many opportunities for data loss, manipulation, or error.

  • üî¥Opaque and difficult to verify
  • üî¥Vulnerable to single points of failure or fraud

Blockchain-Enabled System

All transactions are recorded on a decentralized, shared ledger. Every entry is permanent, time-stamped, and visible to all permissioned participants.

  • üü¢Secure, immutable, and tamper-proof
