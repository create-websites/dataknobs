The Unified Platform for AI Data Products

Dataknobs provides a cohesive, end-to-end solution to build, govern, and optimize data-centric applications, transforming raw data into intelligent, consumable, and valuable assets.

The Intelligent Trio Framework

Our platform is built on three integrated pillars that cover the entire data product lifecycle. This unified approach addresses the fragmentation of modern AI development, providing a single, seamless workflow from creation to optimization.

Products in Action: The Generative Engine

The Kreate module is the generative core of our platform, offering a suite of powerful tools to build the foundational assets of any data product, from high-quality datasets to sophisticated AI agents.

Proven Success Across Industries

Our platform's value and versatility are demonstrated by real-world deployments in demanding sectors. Explore our case studies to see how we deliver measurable business outcomes.

Pragmatic AI Expertise

We focus on the practical, enterprise-grade application of advanced AI. Our expertise lies in helping businesses use cutting-edge technology responsibly and efficiently to move from experimentation to production.

Generative AI & LLMs

Our LLM-agnostic platform and deep focus on Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG) allow us to ground powerful models with your private, proprietary data, ensuring context-aware and secure AI solutions.

Agentic AI in Practice

We build autonomous agents that perceive, reason, and act to achieve goals. From e-commerce analysis to SEO auditing, our agents automate complex analytical tasks, delivering insights without constant human intervention.

Enterprise-Grade Application

Our strength is not in foundational research, but in deploying robust, scalable, and secure AI systems. We provide frameworks and workshops to guide enterprises in applying these technologies effectively while managing risk.

AI Agent for Business Analysis

Analyze reports, dashboard and determine To-do

DataKnobs has built an AI Agent for structured data analysis that extracts meaningful insights from diverse datasets such as e-commerce metrics, sales/revenue reports, and sports scorecards. The agent ingests structured data from sources like CSV files, SQL databases, and APIs, automatically detecting schemas and relationships while standardizing formats. Using statistical analysis, anomaly detection, and AI-driven forecasting, it identifies trends, correlations, and outliers, providing insights such as sales fluctuations, revenue leaks, and performance metrics.
