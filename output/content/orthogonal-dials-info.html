Frontiers of Application

Modern generative AI, causal inference, and large language models rely heavily on orthogonality to tackle complex challenges.

Preserving Knowledge (OFT)

OFT fine-tunes large models for new tasks by rotating weights, avoiding alteration. This maintains pre-trained knowledge and combats "catastrophic forgetting."

Finding True Cause (DML)

DML employs orthogonalization to isolate causal effects, statistically adjusting for confounders in noisy, real-world data.

Steering LLMs (Self-Control)

Emerging methods enable dynamic LLM control during inference. Leveraging latent space gradients, orthogonal "dials" can guide the model's output towards accuracy or desired stylistic tones, eliminating the need for retraining.
