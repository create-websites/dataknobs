Product Vision & Executive Summary

“Simplify city logistics — make moving goods as easy as booking a cab.”

This page specifies the requirements for building an on-demand intra-city logistics platform that allows individuals and small businesses to book 2-wheelers, tempos, and trucks for same-day or scheduled delivery of goods.

MoveGuru platform connects customers with verified driver-partners via a mobile app and web interface, providing real-time pricing, live tracking, secure payments, and digital proof of delivery. The goal is to launch an MVP in one metro city (Bengaluru or Delhi-NCR) and then expand to other Tier-1 cities.

Core Platform Features

Our platform is built on a foundation of powerful, reliable features designed to deliver a best-in-class logistics experience.

Booking & Pricing Engine

Instantly quotes fares based on distance, vehicle, and real-time demand.

Intelligent Driver Matching

Intelligently auto-assigns the nearest available and verified driver to a new job.

Live GPS Tracking

Provides real-time GPS location sharing and dynamic ETA updates for full transparency.

Secure & Multi-Modal Payments

Integrates UPI, card, netbanking, and Cash on Delivery options.

Automated KYC Verification

Ensures all drivers are verified via official documents for safety and trust.

Digital Invoices & ePOD

Generates GST-compliant invoices and digital proof of delivery for every trip.

Multi-Stop Deliveries

Allows users to add multiple drop-off points within a single, optimized trip.

Scheduled Bookings

Gives users the flexibility to book trips for a future date and time.

Our Go-to-Market Roadmap

We are planning a phased rollout to ensure a stable and scalable launch, focusing on validating the core product before aggressive expansion.

1

Pilot (Months 1–3)

Launch the MVP in Bengaluru to validate our core pricing and dispatch algorithms with early adopters.

2

Scale (Months 4–6)

Expand the driver network and launch in two new metro areas (Mumbai, Hyderabad) with a goal of reaching 10,000 trips/day.

3

Growth (Months 6–12)

Onboard SME & business accounts across 6 metro cities, aiming for 100,000 users and achieving positive unit economics.
