Introducing the MoveGuru AI Assistant

Advanced, value-adding capabilities built on top of our core logistics platform to create predictive, conversational, and personalized intelligence.

Smart Customer Experience AI

Transforming the customer journey with predictive suggestions and seamless conversational interfaces.

Predictive Booking Suggestions

AI predicts when and what a user will book next based on their history, improving conversion for repeat bookings.

Natural Language Booking

Customers can say or type commands like “Pick up 3 boxes from Koramangala to Whitefield at 5 PM,” and the AI creates the booking.

Dynamic Fare Transparency

AI explains the fare breakdown in natural language to build trust and reduce disputes.

Adaptive Support Assistant

An LLM-powered chatbot on the app and WhatsApp that understands context to reduce human support costs by up to 60%.

Personalized Offers

Predicts churn risk and sends AI-personalized promo codes to boost retention and lifetime value.

Emotional Tone Adjuster

Chat tone adapts dynamically based on user sentiment for a more human-like interaction.

Driver AI Co-Pilot

Empowering our driver-partners with tools for safer driving, higher efficiency, and better earnings.

Voice Command Interface

Hands-free commands like “Start trip” or “Call customer” for safer driving.

Intelligent Route Advisor

Recommends optimized routes using real-time traffic and historical delay patterns for shorter trips.

Predictive Demand Radar

A heatmap showing where demand will rise next, helping drivers position themselves strategically.

Personalized Earning Goals

AI predicts and nudges drivers on how many trips are needed to meet bonus targets, motivating engagement.

Fraud & Risk Alerts

Detects anomalies like GPS spoofing or idle trips to reduce fraud and improve safety.

Local Language Support

Voice AI available in Hindi, Kannada, Tamil, and more for inclusivity and better adoption.

Operations & Business Intelligence AI

Automating and optimizing backend operations for maximum efficiency and revenue.

Demand Forecasting Engine

An ML model that predicts hourly demand by zone, day, and weather to optimize driver supply.

Auto Surge Pricing Optimizer

A real-time algorithm that adjusts fares by area and demand to maximize revenue.

Dispatch Optimization AI

Assigns the best drivers based on ETA reliability and ratings to reduce cancellations.

Ops Copilot (Natural Language Query)

Allows admins to ask questions like “Show all bookings delayed over 20 mins” and get instant results.

Fraud & Anomaly Detection

Detects patterns of fake bookings or duplicate payments to reduce revenue leakage.

Dynamic Incentive Engine

Auto-creates driver incentives based on real-time demand-supply gaps to improve fulfillment rates.
