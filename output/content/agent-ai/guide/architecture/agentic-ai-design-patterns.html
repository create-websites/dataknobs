The Rise of Agentic AI

Agentic AI describes systems capable of independently sensing their surroundings, reasoning, planning, and acting to meet objectives. Unlike standard models that only react to inputs, agents proactively take action, employ tools, and refine their approach as needed. This part explores the essence of this transformative framework.
