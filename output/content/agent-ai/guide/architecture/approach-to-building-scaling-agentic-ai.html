Prioritizing the First Move

Choosing initial pilot projects is a vital strategic move. Aim for 'Quick Wins' that provide clear benefits, drive progress, and become impactful success stories. Use the matrix to guide project classification effectively.

Quick Wins (Prioritize Now)

High Value, Easy Execution, Quick Wins. A perfect launchpad. Drives tangible results fast, accelerates progress, and reduces uncertainty.

Strategic Bets

High potential, challenging execution. Promising outcomes demand substantial resources. Focus on long-term strategy over short-term trials.

Incremental Gains

Minimal impact, high practicality, low effort. Simple to execute, yet unlikely to drive significant strategic change. Ideal for practice or limited testing scenarios.

Avoid for Now

Low ROI, High Effort/Low Feasibility. Costly with minimal payoff. These initiatives sap resources and energy.
