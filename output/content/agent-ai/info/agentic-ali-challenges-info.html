Navigating Legal Gray Areas

Current legal systems were designed with human participants in mind. Autonomous AI disrupts foundational legal principles like ownership and liability, driving the demand for updated laws. At the heart of this shift lies the concept of 'Legal Agency,' influencing every facet of legal practice.
