Models of Collaboration

The synergy between humans and AI agents can take many forms. Understanding these collaboration models is key to effectively deploying them. Click on each model below to explore its characteristics, typical use cases, and strategic benefits for your organization.

Copilot Model

The agent acts as an assistant, augmenting human tasks and providing suggestions in real-time. The human remains firmly in control.

Specialist Model

Highly-trained agents execute specific, complex workflows autonomously, such as data analysis or code generation.

Autopilot Model

Agents manage and orchestrate entire business processes with high-level human oversight, making decisions and taking actions.
