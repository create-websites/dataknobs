A Heritage in Peril

The unconquered fort now faces its greatest threat: the ravages of time, neglect, and the logistical challenges of conservation.

Structural Decay

Constant saline erosion and the unchecked growth of vegetation are compromising the fort's masonry. In 2023, a portion of the wall collapsed, highlighting the urgent need for scientific conservation and a long-term management plan.

Unregulated Tourism

While vital for the local economy, high visitor traffic without proper infrastructure like a safe jetty contributes to the wear and tear of the fragile structures. Balancing preservation with public access is a critical challenge for its future.
