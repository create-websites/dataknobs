Governing with KONTROLS

KONTROLS provides a holistic, multi-layered framework for AI governance that moves beyond a simple checklist. It ensures every aspect of your data product is secure, compliant, and aligned with business goals. The chart illustrates the six core dimensions of this comprehensive oversight.

  • Data-Centric: Ensures data quality, lineage, and privacy.
  • Model-Centric: Manages bias, performance, and versioning.
  • Output-Centric: Validates results with ethics checks and feedback loops.
  • Infrastructure-Centric: Secures the environment with encryption and cost monitoring.
  • Governance-Centric: Manages policies, audit trails, and access control.
  • Business & User-Centric: Tracks ROI and ensures alignment with user needs.

The "Drive Train" Approach to Building Data Products

This systematic process transforms data from a passive asset into an active driver of actionable results. It is a continuous loop of definition, creation, deployment, and optimization.

1

Define Goal & Levers

Articulate the desired outcome and identify the controllable "knobs" or parameters that can be manipulated to influence it.

2

Collect Data & Build Model

Gather necessary data and develop a model that reflects how the levers and external factors affect the outcome.

3

Process, Deploy & Monitor

Clean data, deploy the model into production, and continuously monitor its performance, accuracy, and reliability.

4

Optimize with "What If" Scenarios

Use simulators to ask "What If" questions, feeding results into an optimizer to find the best solutions and avoid negative outcomes.
