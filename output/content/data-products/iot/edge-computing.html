Edge Computing

  • Latency: Low (Real-time)
  • Bandwidth: Low Requirement
  • Processing: Local (On-Device)
  • Connectivity: Works Offline
  • Best For: Instantaneous response, data privacy, and reliability in remote environments.

Cloud Computing

  • Latency: High
  • Bandwidth: High Requirement
  • Processing: Centralized (Remote)
  • Connectivity: Requires Internet
  • Best For: Large-scale data analysis, complex AI model training, and scalable storage.
