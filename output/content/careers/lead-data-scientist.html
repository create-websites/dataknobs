DataKnobs is a startup building new data products using AI. Our mission is to make data useful and usable. Our solution enable protecting data, anonymizing data, and sharing data with knobs. We enable customers to build data products in well-governed and efficient manner.

Why Join Us

You can make an immediate impact at DataKnobs. We lead with curiosity and aim for the highest and embrace ambiguity as it comes. Join us and make an impact happen with a career at Dataknobs.

Role : Data Scientist

Work location: India (Remote)

Responsibilities:

Responsible for the development of state-of-the-art applied machine-learning projects

Build machine learning models for NLP and structured data

Analyze raw data: assessing quality, cleansing, structuring for downstream processing

Design accurate and scalable prediction model

Collaborate with engineering team to bring analytical prototypes to production

Generate actionable insights for business improvements

You will leverage your strong collaboration skills and ability to extract valuable insights from highly complex data sets to ask the right questions and find the right answers.

Qualifications

Bachelor's degree or equivalent experience in quantitative field (Statistics, Mathematics, Computer Science, Engineering, Data Science etc.)

At least 1 - 2 years' of experience in quantitative analytics or data modeling

Deep understanding of predictive modeling, machine-learning, clustering and classification techniques, and algorithms

Fluency in a programming language (Python, Java, SQL). Knowledge of Terraform

Familiarity with Big Data frameworks and visualization tools (Cassandra, Hadoop, Spark, Tableau)

Preferred Qualifications:

Hands-on experience in one or more of the following areas: machine learning, deep learning, and statistical modeling methods.

Strong coding skills, especially in Python/Java/Go. Knowledge of Cloud..

Familiarity with online experimentation, ab-testing and analytics.

In this position, you will be working with talented machine learning engineers, web developers and data engineers.

Data Product Approach

Why Build Data Products

Companies should build data products because they transform raw data into actionable, reusable assets that directly drive business outcomes. Instead of treating data as a byproduct of operations, a data product approach emphasizes usability, governance, and value creation. Ultimately, they turn data from a cost center into a growth engine, unlocking compounding value across every function of the enterprise.
